Vegetarian Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb plant-based ground meat substitute
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, finely chopped
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb spaghetti, cooked according to package instructions
  • Vegan Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the plant-based ground meat substitute until browned.
  2. Add onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Saut√© until vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, vegetable broth, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Serve the vegetarian Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti. Garnish with vegan Parmesan cheese.
